MFM is open-source and available on GitHub. All features of MFM are available in the free version. You are welcome to contribute by donating your time and working on the source code, improving the user manual or how to guide. If you find a bug, please report it. MFM is free and will be all the time free. If you like MFM you are also very welcome to donate some money.
